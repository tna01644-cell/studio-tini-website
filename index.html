"Studio Tini is where design meets life. I help people discover the value hidden in the tiniest things — proving that minimal stuff isn’t less life, it’s more clarity, more time, more freedom."

I’m Christina Ni. I started Tini Takes Tidying in 2020 and now run Studio Tini, a one-woman studio dedicated to turning chaos into clarity. I feel grateful to help families, young professionals, kids, and anyone seeking a reset in their homes and lives.

Growing up American-born Chinese, I learned resourcefulness and making the most of what we have. Traveling to 19 countries showed me how living light and creating adaptable systems is not just possible — it’s empowering. I approach organization as a joyful reset, helping you reclaim your time, your space, and your mental clarity.

I work with:

  • New homeowners
  • Busy professionals
  • Airbnb hosts
  • People going through life transitions

Whether you’re moving, downsizing, or just want a home that feels easy to maintain, I create functional, beautiful, and maintainable spaces that breathe — and a life where you control your attention in an economy of distraction.
