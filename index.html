The Practice // Christina Ni

Studio Tini is the creative practice of Christina Ni—a name that reflects my obsession with the small, essential details that make a home function.

My background in contract staging and a personal history of traveling to 19 countries has taught me the art of resourcefulness. I don't believe luxury is defined by square footage; it's defined by the logic of the space. Whether I'm working in a high-rise or a studio, my goal is the same: to curate an environment that supports your focus and mental equity.

  • — Resourcefulness over Consumption
