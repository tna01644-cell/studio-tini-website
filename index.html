Professional Organizer & Staging Expert

Studio Tini is Christina Ni—a creative practice focused on the small, essential details that make a home function.

We focus on the essential alchemy of small-footprint living. By merging professional staging with a disciplined approach to organization, we ensure every property doesn't just look ready for market—it feels fundamentally functional.

Based between Denver and Palisade, Studio Tini provides contract staging for residential properties, prioritizing tactile quality and spatial logic.
