Studio Story

I’m Christina Ni. I started Studio Tini to turn chaos into clarity through strategic spatial logic.

Growing up American-born Chinese taught me resourcefulness: making the most of what we already have. Traveling to 19 countries proved that living light is not just possible—it’s empowering.

I approach organization as a joyful reset, helping you reclaim mental equity in your sanctuary.
